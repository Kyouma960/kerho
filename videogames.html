VideoGamesKerho

Lahti's Video Games Hub

Video games evening is hosted fridays from 16:00 - 20:30 at Niemenkatu 73 lobby. Up to 50 people per evening.

8 PC's, 2 PS4, 2 PS3, 40 controllers, racing wheel, jackbox party pack.

In addition, we are organizing a monthly fighting game tournament and LAN party twice a year, with the first one being for 50 people.

Guide for arriving to weekly session

Lahti Science Park
Niemenkatu 73
15140 Lahti
Lobby near the front door reception
https://maps.app.goo.gl/Py8EDD9vp7Rr4HUa7
Enter from the main entrance (revolving door) or from D1. If you arrive before 16:00, you can just walk in. If you arrive after 16:00, you'll need to ask somebody from our WhatsApp & Telegram channels to open the door for you. Links for joining them are in to bottom of this webpage.

About us

Kaptam Gamers started 2024. Our activities are hosted by passionated group of gamers, with experience in video production for companies, building PC's for gaming and playing different types of boardgames for years.

Anybody can join the activities and we will post recent news on Whatsapp & Telegram channels. Links for joining them are in to bottom of this webpage.

If you have an idea for a special event, please email us with your suggestion.
